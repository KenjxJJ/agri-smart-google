# AgriSmart 🌾

**AgriSmart** is a powerful, offline-first agricultural knowledge platform designed to empower local farmers with the information they need to succeed, regardless of their internet connectivity.

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Offline-First Knowledge Base**: Access a core database of crops, diseases, and climate outlooks even without an internet connection.
- **Online Search Integration**: Connect to the [OpenFarm.cc](https://openfarm.cc) API to search for thousands of additional crops when online.
- **Personal Crop Database**: Save crops from online search results directly to your local database for offline access.
- **One-Tap Sync**: Easily update your local database with the latest information from online sources with a single click.
- **Climate Zone Detection**: Use your device's location to receive tailored crop recommendations for your specific climate (Tropical, Temperate, or Arid).
- **Multilingual Support**: Fully translated into **English**, **Hindi (हिन्दी)**, and **Swahili (Kiswahili)** to serve a diverse community of farmers.
- **Disease & Pest Management**: Detailed symptoms and prevention strategies for common crop diseases.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [Next.js 15+](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **Animations**: [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/)
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
- **API**: [OpenFarm API](https://openfarm.cc/api/v1/crops)
- **Storage**: Browser `localStorage` for offline persistence.

## 📖 How to Use

1. **Search for Crops**: Use the search bar to find crops in the local database or search online if connected.
2. **Detect Your Location**: Click "Detect Location" to find your climate zone and see recommended crops for your area.
3. **Save for Offline**: When viewing an online crop result, click "Save to Database" to keep it for offline use.
4. **Update Your Database**: On the home screen, use the sync button to refresh your saved crops with the latest online data.
5. **Change Language**: Use the globe icon in the header to switch between English, Hindi, and Swahili.

## 🌍 Mission

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide in agriculture by providing accessible, reliable, and localized information to farmers everywhere, helping them make informed decisions and improve their yields.
